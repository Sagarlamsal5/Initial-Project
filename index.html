Explore Kathmandu

Hotel Holiday Inn is ideally situated for you to explore the vibrant city of Kathmandu. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage, visit historic sites, and experience the warmth of the local community. Our friendly staff is always ready to provide recommendations and assist you in making the most of your stay.

Rani Pokhari


Tranquil historic pond in Kathmandu. Surrounded by gardens and adorned with a beautiful temple.

Swoyambhu


Iconic Buddhist monument on a hill. Panoramic views, sacred rituals, and ancient carvings.

Boudha


Immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance of Boudhanath Stupa. This magnificent Buddhist stupa, one of the largest in the world, stands as a symbol of peace and enlightenment.

Pashupati


Sacred Hindu temple on Bagmati River. Experience rich rituals and serene atmosphere.

Durbar Square


Explore the exquisite Golden Gate, the iconic Nyatapola Temple, and the serene Bhairava Nath Temple. Bhaktapur Durbar Square offers a captivating journey into the history and artistic grandeur of Nepal.

Basantapur


Step into the historical heart of Kathmandu at Basantapur Durbar Square. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a captivating blend of palaces, temples, and intricate architecture.
